Participant & Finalist @ Various National Competitions

2024 - 2025

Achieved 2nd Prize at the Credtech Hackathon, a national competition hosted by the Programming Club of IIT Kanpur. We developed an end-to-end credit assessment tool using a Flask and XGBoost pipeline to predict issuer credit scores from complex financial and sentiment data. The standout feature was an explainable AI dashboard that visualized the model's reasoning, making risk analysis transparent and accessible.

Selected as Finalists at DIPEX 2025, a 4-day state-level competition hosted by COEP Technological University. We developed and presented Alertica, an AI-powered women's safety application. The platform featured silent SOS activation, smart geofencing for safe-zone alerts, and a critical offline SMS system to ensure functionality in low-connectivity areas. The event challenged us to blend intense coding sprints with continuous pitching to judges and visitors.

At the prestigious Smart India Hackathon 2025, we engineered an integrated safety ecosystem to address the problem statement of "Smart Tourist Safety". Our solution featured a tourist app (Flutter), a police dashboard (React), and a secure blockchain backend (Solidity). The system's core innovation was its AI engine that used Isolation Forest for anomaly detection and a LangChain-powered agentic system to automatically draft verifiable e-FIRs, drastically reducing incident response times.

At the Ideathon hosted by TCET, our team presented an AI-powered portfolio generator designed to streamline personal branding for tech professionals. The platform automates portfolio creation by aggregating project and profile data from sources like GitHub and LinkedIn. The key innovation was its conversational AI editor, which allowed users to customize everything from color palettes to content layout using natural language commands, making web design intuitive and rapid.

Presented our research paper, "Artificial Intelligence in Preventing Environmental Issues," at the MULTICON-W Research Paper Competition 2024. Our work investigated the critical role of AI in combating challenges like pollution and climate change. We analyzed and proposed practical applications, from using predictive analytics for environmental monitoring to deploying AI for optimizing conservation strategies, providing a framework for leveraging technology for a sustainable future.

Secured 3rd place in Codethon, an intense 90-minute competitive programming event hosted on the HackerRank platform. The challenge required designing and implementing efficient solutions for four complex algorithmic problems under significant time pressure, testing our command of data structures, algorithms, and high-speed coding.

Participated in WEHACK'25, a national-level 24-hour hackathon with a strong focus on web3 technologies, supported by industry leaders like Polygon and ETHIndia.
